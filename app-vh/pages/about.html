About us

Our full story

VoltHosting was founded on the 22nd of July 2018, by Rowan. We strive to give all customers the best experience, we do this with our amazing customer service team, and lightning fast speeds!

We host from many locations inside the UK, with datacenters located all around the country. We host multiple things, including Websites, Game servers, and Virtualized servers with a lot more things coming soon!

Passion to serve with excellence

Our mission at VoltHosting is to provide customers with high-end services along with advanced customer support! We focus our price to allow you, the customer, to get the most for the cheapest price.

Personally, we believe everyone should have the freedom to own a server, whether that is to play with your friends or to start a hosting company.

VoltHosting utilises a powerful 10Gbps core network within the United Kingdom. We furthermore provide direct peerings with CloudFlare and Google. This gives us the possibility to provide low latency with offshore clients!
